Team Phoenix Logo

We chose the name Phoenix for our team because, even in the face of failure, we never give up, learn from our mistakes, and rise above the ashes to achieve our objective. Additionally, the colors Orange, Strive, Yellow, and White represent burning ability, winners, happiness, sunshine, rebirth, compassion, and the virtues of knowledge and faith. We utilize the text, pen, and import tools in the vector software to construct our logo. Together, we drew and then designed our logo.

Team Phoenix Goal

Our goal is to win the Steam Olympic award, enthrall people with the projects and work we have completed on our website, and encourage people to love and join our team.

Team Phoenix Skills learnt

We learnt how to design a logo using vector apps, a flowchart and algorithm for our robot code, a trifold for our main presentation and our team Website. We also learnt how to build and code our EV3 robot to complete the 15 superpowered missions.



Team Phoenix Vision

is to live a lasting impression by inspiring others to love and be impressed by our passion and projects. We also want visitors to our website to feel the magic we bring to every endeavor and to join us in fostering genuine appreciation and new innovation that people will genuinely love.




Team Phoenix Mission


Our mission as a team is to persuade people of our age to join our organization and get enthralled with our pursuits. By using our creativity and enthusiasm to engage with people on a deeper level, hoping to create experiences that surpass their expectations.



Team Phoenix Ambition


Our ambition is to Strive for excellence in everything we do, setting high standards and delivering exceptional quality in our project and website. We also create a positive impact on people's lives through your project, making a difference and improving the world around you.
